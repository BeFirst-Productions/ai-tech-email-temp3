What if ship
maintenance could
be predicted before
failures occur?

AI-based predictive maintenance is making it possible. According to recent studies, intelligent monitoring systems can

• Reduce unplanned maintenance costs by up to 30%
• Increase vessel availability by as much as 20%
 
 

These improvements are achieved through the integration of real-time sensor data, digital models, and machine learning algorithms that continually monitor ship performance and detect anomalies in their earliest stages.

Instead of reacting to unexpected failures, fleets can now anticipate them, scheduling maintenance proactively, preventing breakdowns, and delivering more reliable operations across the fleet. The result is lower operating costs, fewer operational disruptions, and heightened safety and confidence onboard.

  
TMS AITECH 2026 will explore how predictive analytics is transforming maritime engineering, vessel operations, and lifecycle management through AI and automation.
